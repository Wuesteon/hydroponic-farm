Materials needed for a vertical hydroponic farm:
- White vinyl fence post
- Two white vinyl pyramid post tops
- 45-gallon tank
- PVC pipe
- Submersible pump
- Light timer
- Net pots
- Hinges
- Screws
- Silicone
- Hose barb tee
- Vinyl hose
- Aluminum pipe

Instructions for building the farm:
- Use PVC pipes, clay balls, a water pump, and nutrient solution to build the farm.
- Use an aluminum sheet for a water splash shield.
- Monitor pH and EC levels in the solution.
