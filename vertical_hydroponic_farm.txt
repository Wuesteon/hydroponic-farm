The article provides step-by-step instructions on how to build a vertical hydroponic tower, also known as a tower garden, using PVC pipes, clay balls, and nutrient solution. The tower can hold 28 plants per tower and two towers can be placed in a 5-foot x 5-foot space, producing 56 plants at one time. The article also lists the materials and tools required and offers post-preparation care tips. Vertical hydroponics has various advantages over traditional crop production methods.
